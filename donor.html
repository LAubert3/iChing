Donor Policy

Your trust is important to us. This Donor Policy explains how we handle your personal and financial information when you make a donation through our platform. By donating, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you make a donation:

  • Your name, email address, and billing address.
  • Payment details (processed securely through our payment provider).
  • Any optional information you provide, such as donation preferences or comments.

How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used for the following purposes:

  • Processing and acknowledging your donation.
  • Sending donation receipts and updates about our work.
  • Meeting legal and regulatory obligations.

We do not sell, trade, or share your financial information with anyone outside our organization and its service providers, except as required by law.

Recurring Donations

For recurring donors, fixed monthly payments will be debited on the monthly anniversary of the first donation. Donations initiated on the 29th, 30th, or 31st of the month will recur on the last day of shorter months, as close to the original date as possible. You may cancel or update your recurring donation at any time by contacting us.

Data Sharing and Security

Your personal and financial data may be shared with trusted third-party service providers to facilitate payment processing and donation management. These providers comply with strict data protection standards to ensure your information is secure.

All payment information is encrypted and processed securely using industry-leading payment gateways. We do not store your financial details on our servers.

Donor Rights

As a donor, you have the following rights:

  • To access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • To opt out of receiving communications from us.
  • To request a record of your donations.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

International Donations

If you are donating from outside the United States, please be aware that your data may be processed and stored in countries with different privacy laws. By donating, you consent to this transfer of data.

Policy Updates

This Donor Policy is subject to change. We will notify donors of significant updates through our website or email. Please review this policy periodically to stay informed.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Donor Policy, please contact us.
