How It Works

Our platform offers two modes for receiving your personalized I Ching readings, both of which are based on the traditional King Wen hexagram sequence. In Manual Mode, you can simulate the coin toss process by selecting the corresponding options for your coin flips. In Auto Mode, we use the random.org API, which generates truly random numbers derived from atmospheric noise, providing a higher level of randomness compared to methods that rely on pseudo-random number generation. Unlike CSPRNGs (Cryptographically Secure Pseudo-Random Number Generators), which use a combination of algorithms and hardware to generate numbers that are deterministic and not truly random, random.org's approach taps into the unpredictability of nature, making it a more reliable source of randomness for your I Ching reading.

Manual Mode:

  • Flip three coins and select the dropdown option that corresponds to your toss, then press 'Enter' to generate a line.
  • Repeat this process six times to generate your hexagram.

Auto Mode:

  • Create an account on random.org to obtain an API Key.
  • Copy and paste the API key into the input box on our platform.
  • Click 'Start' to automatically generate your reading.
